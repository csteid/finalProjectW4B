What is art?

It is difficult to define because words alone cannot represent the way art can affect each individuals response to it.

I think it is easier to define it with quotes from the artists themselves.

  • “I am seeking. I am striving. I am in it with all my heart.” Vincent van Gogh
  • “If I could say it in words there would be no reason to paint.” Edward Hopper
  • “Art is not what you see, but what you make others see.” Edgar Degas
  • “It took me four years to paint like Raphael, but a lifetime to paint like a child.” Pablo Picasso
  • “I found I could say things with color and shapes that I couldn't say any other way--things I had no words for.” Georgia O'Keeffe

Leonardo da Vinci’s “Mona Lisa” is considered to be the most famous painting of all time.
She attracts 10 million visitors a year to the Lourve Museum in Paris.
